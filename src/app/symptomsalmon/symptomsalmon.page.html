Symptom/ Signs

Symptoms of Salmonella poisoning usually begin 12-72 hours after infection which is:


  • Diarrhea

The diarrhea is typically loose and not bloody.

  • Abdominal cramping
  • Fever
  • Nausea
  • Vomiting
  • Headache
  • Muscle aches

The symptoms usually go away on their own after four to seven days.
